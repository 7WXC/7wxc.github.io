About

Hi! I am Wei Shao, a master's student at Zhejiang University, advised by Dr. Ziquan Fang and Prof. Yunjun Gao.

My research focuses on Data-Centric AI at the intersection of data mining and data governance. I am particularly interested in LLM pre-training data, trajectory intelligence, multivariate time-series cleaning, and dataset condensation.

I have published papers at leading venues including ICML, SIGMOD, ICLR, and KDD, and received the SIGMOD Student Travel Grant and KDD Student Travel Award. Beyond research, I have gained industry experience at Ubiquant Investment and Bosch.

I expect to graduate in June 2027 and am actively seeking full-time opportunities.
